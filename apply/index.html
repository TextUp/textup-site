What is TextUp?

TextUp is a text messaging platform seeking to improve the outcomes of the homeless and near-homeless served by social service providers through improved channels of communication. By aggregating information on a consolidated platform, TextUp enables providers (e.g., shelters and meal sites) to report and adjust service availabilities as well as send text alerts with pertinent information. TextUp allows constituents to access this data in real time for informed decision-making.

TextUp was one of the ventures in the 2014 cohort of the Social Innovation Initiative. We are currently supported by grants from VentureWell in Massachusetts and the United Way of Rhode Island to build and test a prototype. We are part of the present cohort of ventures in the Social Enterprise Greenhouse Accelerator program.

Community Engagement Coordinator

Description

The Community Engagement Coordinator is responsible for engaging social service providers and homeless individuals to test and evaluate possible service features and collect and integrate feedback. This person will work closely with product designers and programmers to determine which features are promising and should be further developed.

This person must be:

  • Resourceful, in order to find new ways to get in touch with and keep in touch with busy service providers and hard-to-find homeless constituents;
  • Proactive, in order to conduct field work and interview case managers, homeless constituents, and agency directors either alone or with other members of the team;
  • Engaged, in order to shape which features and strategies we pursue in light of our customer research;
  • Organized, in order to collect and report all feedback from interviews; and
  • Dedicated, in order to commit growing and developing the venture.

Scope of Work

Commitment is 5-15 hrs/week (flexible). Possibility for extension into a funded summer fellowship. The Community Engagement Coordinator will be expected to:

  • Take ownership of the customer discovery process and delegating work to other members of the team
  • Research with which organizations and individuals TextUp should engage and proceed accordingly
  • Organize and analyze transcripts and qualitative and quantitative data from the field
  • Brainstorm and ideate with other members of the team to determine how our customer interviews will affect current, planned and future features
  • Build relationships with case managers and service providers to convince them to test our product
  • Work with other members of the team to develop and implement and test strategy, determining effectiveness and usefulness of a specific feature
  • Conduct interviews and site visits
  • Train new members on customer interviewing best practices

Qualifications

  • Existing interest in homelessness, poverty, income equality, and social justice
  • Experience with community work, grassroot activism, or community interviewing work
  • Ability and confidence to cold-call service providers, explain our purpose, and begin to build up relationships
  • Ability to interview homeless and near homeless constituents to learn more about existing pain points
  • Interest and willingness to contribute to the ideation and product development iterations and user testing best practices
